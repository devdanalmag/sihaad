Our Vision & Mission

Driving technological literacy, financial freedom, and global inclusion

Our Vision

To make Sihaad Nigerian Limited the hub of blockchain and Web3 innovation in Africa, driving technological literacy, financial freedom, and global inclusion. We want to position Africa as not just a user of Web3 technology, but a creator, innovator, and global contributor.

Our Mission

  • To democratize access to blockchain, crypto, and Web3 education.
  • To guide and mentor individuals in safely navigating decentralized technologies.
  • To create opportunities for Africans to participate in the digital economy.
  • To empower communities through collaboration and innovation.
  • To develop solutions that solve real-world problems using science and technology.
